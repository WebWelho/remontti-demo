# Remontti Työrönkainen – Design Brainstorm

## Approach A — Nordic Craft (CHOSEN)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Nordic Craft Modernism — Scandinavian minimalism meets artisan warmth.

**Core Principles:**
1. Light, airy backgrounds (warm white, stone, linen) — fresh and inviting, not cold.
2. Warm amber/terracotta accent — signals craftsmanship, quality, and warmth.
3. Asymmetric editorial layouts — sections break out of the grid for visual interest.
4. Generous whitespace as a design element — breathing room signals confidence.

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `oklch(0.98 0.008 85)` — warm off-white/linen
- Foreground: `oklch(0.18 0.02 60)` — deep charcoal-brown (not pure black)
- Primary accent: `oklch(0.62 0.13 55)` — warm amber/terracotta
- Section alt: `oklch(0.94 0.012 85)` — stone/cream
- Dark section: `oklch(0.16 0.02 250)` — deep navy for contrast sections

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial — hero extends to top edge with transparent nav, services in a horizontal scroll slider on mobile / 3-col grid on desktop, portfolio in masonry-style, process as a horizontal timeline.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin amber rule lines separating sections and decorating headings.
2. Large serif numerals (Fraunces) as decorative counters and step numbers.
3. Photo cards with subtle warm overlay on hover.

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, purposeful — fade-up on scroll, card lifts on hover, navbar transitions from transparent to frosted glass.

**Animation:** Staggered fade-up (0.6s ease, 24px translateY), counter animation on stats, slider with embla-carousel.

**Typography System:**
- Display/headings: Fraunces (serif, variable weight 300–900) — editorial gravitas.
- Body: DM Sans (400, 500, 600) — clean, modern, legible.
- Accent labels: DM Sans 700 uppercase letter-spacing 0.15em.
